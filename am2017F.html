Art of Making F2016

This course is a hands-on introduction to the technology and mindsets of making. Projects will introduce students to coding, engineering, and design thinking by leveraging tools like Arduinos, 3D printers, laser cutters, and more. Projects include:

  1. Electronic Music Instruments: In Project 1 students demonstrate proficiency with Arduino programming fundamentals by developing electronic music instruments.
  2. ROBOlympics: Students compete in various robotic events (ping pong pickup, catapault, maze, etc.) with Arduino-controlled robots.
